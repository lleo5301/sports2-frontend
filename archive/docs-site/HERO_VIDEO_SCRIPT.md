# Hero Video Script — "Welcome to Sports2"

> 2–3 minute walkthrough for first-time users. Record with Loom, OBS, or Screen Studio.

---

## Opening (0:00–0:20)

**On screen:** Dashboard or login screen.

**Script:**
> "Welcome to Sports2—the platform for collegiate baseball scouting and team management. In this short video, we'll walk you through the basics so you can get started quickly."

---

## Login (0:20–0:35)

**Action:** Show login screen (or skip if starting from dashboard).

**Script:**
> "Log in with your email and password. If you're new, your coach or administrator will have sent you an invite."

---

## Dashboard Tour (0:35–1:15)

**Action:** Hover/point to each area while speaking.

**Script:**
> "Once you're in, you'll land on your dashboard. Here you see your team's branding, key stats—players, prospects, your record—and recent or upcoming games. Use the sidebar to navigate. Roster lets you manage players and rosters. Recruiting is for prospects and your recruiting board. Scouting is for reports. Operations has schedules, games, the coach dashboard, and depth charts."

---

## Quick Demo: One Flow (1:15–2:20)

**Action:** Choose one simple flow—e.g. open Players, show the list, click one player, show the detail. Keep it short.

**Script:**
> "Let's take a quick look at the roster. [Click Players.] Here's your full roster. [Click a player.] Each player has a profile with stats, depth chart placement, and more. You can add players, create rosters, and build depth charts—including filling a chart from a game lineup. We'll cover those in detail in the guides."

---

## Closing (2:20–2:45)

**Action:** Return to dashboard or show docs link.

**Script:**
> "For step-by-step guides on every feature, check the Sports2 User Guide in the docs. Thanks for watching—we're here to help you win."

---
