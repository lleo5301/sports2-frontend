# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Account & Login

**I forgot my password.**  
Use **Forgot password** on the login page to reset it via email.

**I can't log in.**  
Double-check your email and password. Ensure caps lock is off. Contact your administrator if the problem continues.

**I don't have an account.**  
Contact your head coach or administrator to be invited.

---

## Data & Features

**I don't see a feature (e.g., depth charts, schedule edit).**  
Your role or permissions may not include that feature. Contact your head coach or administrator.

**How do I switch teams?**  
Use the team selector in the sidebar or top bar. You’ll only see it if you have access to multiple teams.

**Where does my data live?**  
All data is scoped to your team and is isolated from other teams. It is stored securely and managed by your organization.

---

## Integrations

**How do I connect PrestoSports?**  
Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar and follow the setup steps. You’ll need your PrestoSports credentials. An integrations guide is coming soon.

---

## Support

**I need help.**  
Reach out to your head coach or administrator. For technical issues, they can escalate to Sports2 support.
